maxchem
/datum/plant_gene/trait/maxchem

A plant trait that causes the plant's capacity to double.

When harvested, the plant's individual capacity is set to double it's default. However, the plant is also going to be limited to half as many products from yield, so 2 yield will only produce 1 plant as a result.
